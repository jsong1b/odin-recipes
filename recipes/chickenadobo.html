(Famous) Chicken Adobo

Description

Savory, tangy, with a rich dark brown sauce coating tender, slow-cooked chicken. What is it? It’s the Philippines’ national dish, Chicken Adobo — a perfect medley of salty, sour, and umami flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 (3 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, quartered and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2/3 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat and cook chicken pieces until golden brown. Once cooked, transfer the chicken to a plate and set aside.
  2. Add onion and garlic to skillet and brown them.
  3. Pour soy sauce and vinegar with garlic powder, black pepper, and bay leaf onto skillet.
  4. Return the chicken to the pan and incrase heat to high, bringing the sauce to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until chicken is tender and cooked through.
